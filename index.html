Teaching Platform

AIET adopts student centric approach in its pedagogic practices such as
Class-room learning : Teachers deliver lectures using multi-media such as PPTs, Videos, and Recorded Lectures and also, use case study analysis to improve classroom dynamics. They strictly adhere to the University prescribed syllabus and norms in conducting classes, examinations, evaluations and assessments. Curriculum gaps addressed through technical talks, workshops, seminars and conferences. Presently, the classes are being conducted on-line on Impartus Platform, Google Classroom, Moodle depending on the comfort and convenience of the learner and the teacher.

To enhance learning potential of students innovative techniques are adopted such as-
Cross-over learning : Learning in informal settings, such as experiential learning in companies, working in research oriented organizations and after-class clubs, can connect educational content with issues that matter to learners in their academic pursuits. These connected experiences have found to spark further interest and motivation to learn.

Learning through argumentation : Teachers encourage students to ask open-ended questions, participate in discussions, look for answers from authoritative secondary sources and learn from experts.

Incidental learning : AIET provides plenty of opportunities for students to organize interdepartmental and inter-collegiate activities. Unplanned or unintentional learning occurs while planning, organizing and carrying out an activity that is seemingly unrelated to what is learned.

Learning by doing : AIET encourages and guides the students to take part in mini-projects, Seminars, Internships, Certification courses, Student development programs (SDP) in the trending fields of technology, enhancing their career prospects. There is an innovation lab where students are encouraged to incubate startups. Management and faculty are always there to support student initiatives.

Mentor/Mentee Program
Among the best practices adopted by AIET Mentor, the Mentee System has proved highly effective. Each lecturer is assigned as a mentor for students to guide them in their studies and help them to become familiar with the Institute culture. The Mentor meets the mentees on a regular basis. All papers concerning the mentees are available with the Mentors for quick reference and facilitation.

Accreditation & MOU’s

Alva’s Institute of Engineering and Technology has been awarded NBA accreditation for its Department of Computer Science Engineering and Department Electronics and Communication Engineering for the academic year 2019-’20 and 2020-’21. NBA accreditation is a process of quality assurance and improvement, whereby an engineering programme in an approved Institution is critically appraised to verify that the Institution or the programme continues to meet and/or exceed the Norms and Standards prescribed by NBA Board, the regulator from time to time. It is a kind of recognition which indicates that a programme or Institution fulfills desired standards. AIET has signed several MOUs with India’s leading Centres for Advancement of Science and Technology, Research Organizations, Industry Associations, Centres of Excellence and Corporates to collaborate on academic and student initiatives, share best practices and generate research studies. The Memorandum of Understanding has facilitated exchange of students and faculty, collaboration on research projects, internships for engineering and post-graduate students, organize technical talks, seminars, workshops and conferences enabling exchange of academic information and materials.

About AIET

Alva’s Education Foundation® (AEF) was born out of the entrepreneurial vision of Dr M Mohan Alva, an ayurvedic doctor, an educationalist and a social reformer in Moodubidri, South India. Alva’s Institute of Engineering and Technology is a premier engineering college situated in Mijar was founded in 2008 by AEF to provide affordable quality professional education to the youth of this underdeveloped region.

Established in 2008, AIET has launched path-breaking initiatives in Indian higher education with the introduction of innovative and modern curricula, insistence on academic discipline, imparting of Holistic Education and adoption of global higher education practices with the support of creative and dedicated staff. The Institute is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi and recognised by AICTE and University Grants Commission (UGC) of India. In the last academic year two of its departments Electronics and Communication Engineering and Computer Science Department have secured NBA accreditation.

AIET in its endeavour to excel in teaching, research and service offers Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, IT Enabled Services Engineering, and Civil Engineering. AIET also offers a post graduate degree in Business Administration.

To be eligible for admission to B. Tech program in Alva’s Institute of Engineering and Technology (affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University), candidates must have passed class 12 with Physics and Chemistry with minimum 45% marks (40% marks for SC/ ST). The selection is done on the basis of the written test by KCET.

To be eligible for admission to MBA program in Alva’s Institute of Engineering and Technology (affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University), candidates must have passed the qualifying graduate degree in any discipline with minimum 50% marks (45% marks for SC/ST). The selection is done on the basis of a written test in the KMAT/GMAT/CAT exam.

Merit cum Means Scholarship is available to minority community students (Muslims, Jains, Christians, Parsis and Zorastrians) and Karnataka E Pass scholarship available for OBC students pursuing engineering and management studies. Moreover, Alva’s Education Foundation awards scholarships to Meritorious and deserving students.

Research & Publications

In an environment where the mindset of students is set on landing in a good job, and where research and publications take a back seat, AIET has made it compulsory for every student to write critical review papers or carry out empirical studies in the field of their interest. Faculty promotions are also tied to publishing papers in UGC recognised or scopus indexed journals. Workshops are conducted to train faculty and students to write and publish papers in reputed journals.

Labs

AIET is well-equipped with state-of-art lab facilities for its engineering students. They are: The Digital System Design Lab enables students to analyze and design digital circuits. The Communication Systems Lab facilitates practicals related to digital communication, analog communication, fiber optics, and telecommunications; The Microprocessor & Microcontroller Lab enables students to learn the architecture, programming and interfacing of these devices. The lab facilitates development of projects. The lab has XELTEK universal programmer to program the ICs; The VLSI Lab provides practical understanding of theoretical concepts studied in CMOS VLSI Design, Microelectronics Circuits and HDL subjects with the help of Cadence tool framework; The Power Electronics Lab introduces students to various devices used in industries. Students can explore the characteristics and use of these devices; The Electrical Lab is well equipped to cater to the curricular needs of students.

Guest Lectures and Visitors: Important guests and visitors and their Visions

In this dynamic Industrial scenario, the curriculum needs to be updated regularly to meet the emerging needs of the industry and the profession. This purpose is achieved to a certain extent through guest lectures and workshops which help to keep pace with the technological advances. Minimum three guest lectures are to be held for every class in each semester. Eminent personalities and experts drawn from various fields, leading businessmen, industrialists, Chief Executives of financial institutions, Heads of other renowned academic institutions and eminent researchers drawn from science and social sciences are invited to speak to the students. The students also get opportunities to interact with the people in the industrial world and gain knowledge and sound exposure to face the world.

Industrial Visits & Social awareness program

Industrial visits are mandated by VTU to lessen the gap between classroom lessons and practical working environment in the corporates. AIET holds one outside the state industrial tour for a week at least during the academic year and local industrial visits every month for students to get an overview of the internal working of companies. Industry visits sensitize students to the practical challenges an industry or organization faces every day. It also helps students to plan their careers.

Healthy and fulfilling social connections can bring out the best in humanity. AIET being in rural settings has plenty of opportunities to reach out to the neighbouring Government schools, primary health centres, meet farmers and small business people and share with them their knowledge, abilities and skills. The NSS, NCC, and several other clubs are continuously engaged in some social service with high impact.

As many as 2,011 post-graduate and under-graduate students studying under Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Visveswaraya Technological University, Belagavi and Mangalore University, Mangaluru received degree certificates at the 18th graduation day of Alva’s Education Foundation, on May 04, 2019 at K V Subbanna Amphitheatre, Vidyagiri.

Traditional Day
Traditional Day hosted at AIET for all institutions of Alva’s Education Foundation is a day that will ever be remembered for its elegance, beauty, grandeur and unity in diversity. There are multi-regional food and snack stalls managed by students and faculty offering exotic foods of their cuisine. The last traditional day was celebrated on March 30, 2019. Smt. B Jayashree a renowned and well loved theatre artiste, Padmashree awardee adorned the dais as the Chief Guest.
